Compounding Nature of These Crises

These four crises interact. Fewer new patients (Crisis 1) reduces the revenue base available to absorb collection failures (Crisis 2). Uninsured visits (Crisis 3) worsen collection outcomes. Misclassified patients (Crisis 4) consume high-cost resources without appropriate billing, compressing margins further. Addressing them in isolation understates the total recovery potential — integrated response amplifies returns across all four.
