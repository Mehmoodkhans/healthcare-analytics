The Strategic Paradox

This organisation simultaneously demonstrates exceptional clinical quality (11.36% readmission rate — top 10% nationally, $303K average patient LTV) and four structural crises that are compounding in real time. The crises are not clinical failures — they are operational and financial system failures sitting on top of clinical excellence.

Why the Opportunity Is $13.3B

The $13.3B is not speculative. Every dollar is grounded in specific patient counts, billing records, and payment transactions already in the database. The investment to unlock it is $3.8M — primarily analytical activation of infrastructure that is already built, validated, and production-ready.

Why This Schema Matters

Every finding in this portfolio required navigating referential relationships across multiple tables simultaneously. A revenue cycle finding required joining VIST → BILL → INSR across 1.4M+ rows. A safety finding required PTAL → ALGY → MDCN cross-checks. The 5-table JOIN (PATN → APPT → VIST → BILL → TRTM) required index design to execute in under 1 second — without it, the same query runs in 21.9 seconds.

Technical Achievement at Scale

Working across 17 tables, 7.08M rows, and 24 months of data required managing data quality issues, NULL handling, synthetic data artefacts, and performance constraints at every stage. 26 strategic indexes covering 82% of query paths — well above the 40–60% industry standard — ensure every analytical question in this portfolio can be answered in production time.
